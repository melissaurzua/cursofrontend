Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.



Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletes through the building og character, seld-discipline, and leadership.



General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in whicj to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhoods. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league memebers.
